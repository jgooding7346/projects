Minecraft Space Invader Hunt - James Gooding - 2014

1. Load a Minecraft world. Ideally create a new one as severe damage may be dealt to your world.
2. Open the program in Python 2 IDLE (Python 3 is incompatible with McPi)
3. Press run

  i. Go to the coordinate shown in chat.
  ii. You will have 60 seconds.
  iii. When at the Space Invader stand directly next to the very centre of this (block placing may be required).
